Aims

  • To make God's good news to known to children, young people and families.
  • To encourage people of all ages to meet God daily through the bible and prayer so that they may come to personal faith with our Lord Jesus Christ

Objectives

  • To provide an effective avenue for mission to students on campus
  • To provide a forum for continuity as they enroll in higher institutions
  • To encourage young people in the campuses to know and practice God's word to grow in Christian maturity and discipleship.
  • To provide a means of encouraging active participation of members of SUCF in SU work
